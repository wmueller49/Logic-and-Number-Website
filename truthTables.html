Truth Tables

p: T T F F | q: T F T F

To generate a table, type in the table you want created

You can use p, q, (), !, &&, and ||

You MUST put parenthesis around your truth table and DO NOT put any spaces

Valid: (p||q), (p&&(p||q)), !(p&&q)

Invalid: p||q, p || q
